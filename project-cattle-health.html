Tracking and Monitoring Cattle’s Health Using Wireless Sensor Networks

April 2025

Project Overview

Designed and developed an IoT-enabled system to monitor vital parameters of cattle using Wireless Sensor Networks (WSN). This innovative solution aims to improve livestock management by providing real-time insights into animal health, facilitating early detection of health issues and timely medical intervention.

Key Implementations

  • IoT Integration: Integrated sensors (temperature, pulse), a GPS module, and IoT connectivity to provide real-time data and instant alerts.
  • Real-time Monitoring: The system continuously tracks and reports vital signs, ensuring immediate visibility into the cattle's well-being.
  • GPS Tracking: Location tracking capabilities allow for monitoring the cattle's movement and location.

The system was successful in monitoring and reporting critical health parameters, demonstrating strong technical and problem-solving skills in the field of Internet of Things (IoT) and embedded systems.
